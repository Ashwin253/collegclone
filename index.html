College Vidya Existence

India has a net of 9.6 Million students that will enroll in distance education by the end of 2021. Still, the distance education sector in India is unorganized and students face a lot of difficulties in getting information on it. College Vidya aims to tackle the current difficulties of students. College Vidya is India's first online platform that brings you all the distance universities at a single platform.
